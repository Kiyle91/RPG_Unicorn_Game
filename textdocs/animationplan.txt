CORE ANIMATION GOALS
────────────────────
• Soft, colorful, and friendly animations to appeal to young players.  
• Visual feedback for every button press and game action.  
• Create a warm, storybook-like feeling through smooth motion and glow effects.  

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SCREEN-BY-SCREEN ANIMATION CONCEPTS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

① LANDING PAGE
   • Title fades in with sparkles.  
   • Start button shimmers gently.  
   • Background clouds move slowly across the screen.  

② NAMING PAGE
   • Input field glows softly when active.  
   • Confirm button triggers sparkly transition to next page.  

③ CLASS SELECTION PAGE
   • Character icons bounce slightly on hover.  
   • Selection pulses when chosen.  
   • Page transitions with a sparkle fade.  

④ DIFFICULTY SELECTION PAGE
   • Buttons slide in from the sides.  
   • Confirmation causes a gentle glow or pop effect.  

⑤ STORY PAGES
   • Text fades in smoothly, like turning a storybook page.  
   • Subtle background movement (sparkles or floating lights).  
   • Ambient sparkles or gentle glows appear between paragraphs.  

⑥ BATTLE SCREEN
   • Attack animations:
        → Sword slash (melee).  
        → Spell glow (magic).  
        → Healing sparkles (heal action).  
   • Parry mechanic shows a brief shield animation for math success.  

⑦ GAME OVER / VICTORY
   • Victory → confetti burst, bounce animation, unicorn sparkle effect.  
   • Game Over → slow fade-in with soft sound cue and retry button glow.  

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IMPLEMENTATION STYLE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Use **CSS animations** for lightweight visuals.  
• Trigger effects through **JavaScript** for interactive timing and sequencing.  
• Use **SVG**, **GIF**, or **Lottie JSON** assets for clean, scalable visuals.  
• Keep animations responsive — avoid lag on lower-end devices.
