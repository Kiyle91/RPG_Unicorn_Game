           ★  RPG BATTLE SYSTEM – OLIVIA’S WORLD  ★
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CLASSES & LEVELING
────────────────
• WARRIOR → Melee-focused, faster HP and melee level-up  
• MAGE → Spell-focused, faster mana and spell level-up  
• HEALER → Moderate melee, healing focus (more efficient heals)  

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BATTLE FLOW
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
① INITIATIVE ROLL  
   • Determines who attacks first (player or enemy).  

② ENEMY ATTACK PHASE  
   • Enemy attacks.  
   • Player can *parry* by answering a math question within a time limit.  

③ PLAYER TURN OPTIONS  
   • **Attack / Spell / Heal:**  
        → Chance for critical damage (x2) if math question answered correctly.  
        → Otherwise, normal damage or healing is applied.  
   • **Flee:** Returns to class menu.  

④ LOOP CONDITION  
   • Continues until:  
        → Enemy HP = 0 → Victory  
        → Player HP = 0 → Game Over  

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GAME OVER
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Display “Game Over” text.  
• Continue button → Returns to class menu.  

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SPECIAL BATTLE MECHANIC – “OPTIONAL PASS”
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Player can skip a math question when prompted.  
• **Availability Rules:**  
     → Can only be used after every 3 correct answers.  
     → Track count until skip ability recharges.  
     → Player can use up to 5 total passes per game.  

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DIFFICULTY SCALING
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• **EASY** → Simpler math questions, lower enemy HP.  
• **MEDIUM** → Moderate difficulty and HP.  
• **HARD** → Complex math challenges, stronger enemies.  

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BOSS FIGHT – “THE EVIL SORCERER” 🧙‍♂️✨
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STORY INTEGRATION
   • The boss fight concludes the tutorial and acts as the story climax.  
   • Storyline example:  
     “The evil sorcerer has captured the unicorn! Defeat him to save your friend!”  
   • Introduced on a story page with suspenseful animations before the battle.  

BOSS STATS & MECHANICS
   • HP: ~150 (significantly higher than normal enemies).  
   • Attack Type: Mix of melee and magic.  
   • Special Moves:
        → Fireball (magic attack)  
        → Dark Swipe (heavy melee strike)  
   • Turn Mechanics:
        → Boss attacks every turn.  
        → Occasionally uses special attack for added challenge.  
   • Visual Effects: Flashing glow, larger character sprite, sparkles, and shadow aura.  

BATTLE FLOW (BOSS)
   1. **Player Turn**
        → Attack / Spell / Heal / Flee  
        → Math questions still apply for critical hits.  
        → Skip ability available if previously earned.  
   2. **Boss Turn**
        → Random attack or special move.  
   3. **Victory**
        → Boss defeated animation triggers.  
        → Story concludes with victory dialogue.  
        → Player earns their first badge or reward for completing the tutorial.  

BOSS FIGHT ANIMATIONS
   • **Entrance:** Dramatic fade-in, large shadow, glowing eyes.  
   • **Attacks:**  
        → Fireball: Magic projectile animation.  
        → Melee: Large slash GIF with flash effect.  
   • **Critical Hits:** Comic-style “BAM!” overlay and screen shake.  
   • **Victory:** Confetti explosion, unicorn flies in, rainbow sparkle animation.  
   • **Defeat:** Soft fade to black with playful “Try Again!” message.  

IMPLEMENTATION TIPS
   • Create separate **boss.css** and **boss.js** files for modular structure.  
   • Use a *flag variable* so the tutorial boss triggers only once.  
   • Add collectibles or stars as post-battle rewards.  
   • Keep assets light — use SVGs, Lotties, or compressed GIFs for animation.  

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
NOTES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Keep combat logic modular for easy balancing and testing.  
• Math challenge difficulty scales with selected difficulty mode.  
• Encourage replay value through random math questions and optional rewards.
